## Major Features

We are pleased to announce the **March release of Aspose.SVG for .NET 26.3.0**!

### Highlights

| Feature / Change | Benefit |
|------------------|---------|
| **Improved SVG to PDF generation** | Enhanced stability and quality of SVG to PDF conversion, with internal improvements focused on rendering and output generation. |
| **General internal refinements** | Applied maintenance updates and internal optimizations to improve reliability of the conversion pipeline. |

## Enhancements and Fixes

- Improved SVG to PDF generation with internal refinements in rendering and document output.
- Enhanced overall reliability and consistency of the SVG to PDF conversion pipeline.
- Performed general maintenance and internal quality improvements.

No public API surface changes were introduced in this release.
