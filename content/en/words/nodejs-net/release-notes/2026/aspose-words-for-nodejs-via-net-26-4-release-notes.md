## Major Features

There are 50 improvements and fixes in this regular monthly release. The most notable are:


- **PDF Export:** Improved the PDF logical structure by exporting node custom IDs to custom attributes.
- **Charts:** Added support for rendering [data labels](https://reference.aspose.com/words/nodejs-net/aspose.words.drawing.charts/chartdatalabelcollection/) within funnel charts.
- **Charts:** Added support for applying chart style settings to leader line rendering.
- **Charts:** Added support for locale IDs when rendering default axis titles in DrawingML charts.
